  • Faqs
  • What services does LeafLand offer?
    LeafLand offers a wide range of landscaping services, including garden maintenance, hardscaping, tree and shrub pruning, water features, landscape lighting, and seasonal cleanups.
  • Do you offer customized landscape designs?
    Yes, we provide personalized landscape designs tailored to your preferences and the unique conditions of your property. Our team works closely with you to bring your vision to life.
  • How do I schedule a consultation?
    You can schedule a consultation by contacting us through our website or by calling our office. We’ll arrange a convenient time to visit your property and discuss your landscaping needs.
  • What areas do you serve?
    LeafLand serves residential and commercial clients in and around Cheyenne, and certain states like Ohio. If you are unsure if we cover your area, please contact us for confirmation.
  • How often should I schedule garden maintenance?
    The frequency of garden maintenance depends on your landscape’s needs and your preferences. We offer flexible maintenance plans ranging from weekly to monthly services to keep your garden healthy and beautiful.
  • Are your services environmentally friendly?
    Yes, we prioritize sustainable practices in all our services, from using eco-friendly materials to implementing water-saving irrigation systems. We aim to create landscapes that are both beautiful and environmentally responsible.
