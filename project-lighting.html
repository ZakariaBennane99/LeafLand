Greenwich Garden

Supervised By Jack Thompson
  • CLIENT:Greenwich Family
  • SUPERVISOR:Jack Thompson
  • LOCATION:Lawrenceville, GA
  • CATEGORY:Landscape Lighting

We enhanced the outdoor environment of the Greenwich family home with our landscape lighting project in Lawrenceville, GA. Led by our skilled supervisor, Jack Thompson, we focused on delivering exceptional quality and meticulous attention to detail.

Collaborating with the Greenwich family, we designed and executed a comprehensive landscape lighting plan that featured:

  • Stylish and energy-efficient lighting fixtures
  • Strategic illumination of key landscape elements
  • Pathway lighting for safety and visual appeal
  • Customized lighting solutions to create a captivating nighttime ambiance

The outcome was a remarkable transformation that not only beautified the property but also enhanced its functionality, providing the Greenwich family with a stunning outdoor space to enjoy day and night.
