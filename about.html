Our Story & Founder

LeafLand was born out of a passion for nature and a vision to create beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces. Founded in 2004 by Jemie Henders, LeafLand has grown from a small, local business to a leading name in the landscaping industry, known for its commitment to excellence and innovative approach. Our missions have always been the same.
  • Create Beautiful, Sustainable Landscapes
  • Deliver Exceptional Service With Quality & Innovation
  • Bring Clients' Visions to Life, Exceeding Expectations
  • Innovate & Improve Continually
